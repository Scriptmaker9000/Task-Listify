Privacy Policy

The information we collect is used solely for website and app improvements. By understanding how users interact with the app, the devices they use, and their preferences, we can optimize performance, improve user experience, and ensure that the app works effectively across different platforms.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to enhance and optimize app performance, provide essential features, and address bugs or improve user experience based on aggregated usage data. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

4. Storage and Security

Your to-do list data is stored locally on your device. No data is uploaded to our servers. While we strive to use secure methods, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

Our application does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or other services that collect personal data.

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your to-do list data stored on your device. Deleting the app or its local files will permanently remove your data.
